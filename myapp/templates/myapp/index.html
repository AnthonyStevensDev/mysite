About Me

From IT Infrastructure
to Software Engineering

With over ten years of experience in IT infrastructure and support, I have developed a strong understanding of how systems, networks, and technology work at a fundamental level. That foundation gives me an advantage as I transition into software engineering — I do not just write code, I understand the environment it runs in.

I am currently teaching myself Python development and AI engineering by building real world projects that solve genuine problems. Rather than following tutorials, I am deliberately choosing projects that push my skills forward — including an AI powered document summariser, a sentiment analysis dashboard, a job application assistant, a predictive analytics tool, and a real time price comparison scraper. I am early in this journey but I am building in public, solving real problems, and improving every day. If you are looking for someone hungry to grow, comfortable with infrastructure, and serious about Python and AI — I would love to connect.
